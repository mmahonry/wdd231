NP Membership Benefits

  • Free membership for qualified non-profit organizations
  • Directory listing with NP badge
  • Access to community events and trainings
  • Opportunity to collaborate on community projects

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Directory listing and website link
  • Invitations to networking events
  • Member-only newsletter & updates
  • Eligibility for basic sponsorships

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Home page spotlight opportunities
  • Discounts on chamber workshops & trainings
  • Social media promotion twice per year

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits
  • Priority spotlight on home page & directory
  • VIP access to signature events
  • Opportunities to sponsor major initiatives
